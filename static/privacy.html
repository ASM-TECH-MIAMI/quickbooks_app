Data We Access

The application accesses QuickBooks Online data (financial reports, transactions, invoices, and company information) through the Intuit OAuth 2.0 API solely to generate business insights for the authorized account holder.

Data is used exclusively to answer financial questions and generate reports for internal business management. We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored securely in an encrypted database. Conversation history is retained for session continuity and can be deleted upon request.

Your Rights
